UpWord provides RSS feeds to help you stay updated with daily English articles. You can subscribe to the full feed or specific difficulty levels based on your learning needs.

## Available Feeds

We offer the following RSS feeds:

| Name | Content | URL |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **All Levels** | Includes Easy, Medium, and Hard articles | `https://upword.day/rss.xml` |
| **Easy** | Only Easy level articles | `https://upword.day/rss/easy` |
| **Medium** | Only Medium level articles | `https://upword.day/rss/medium` |
| **Hard** | Only Hard level articles | `https://upword.day/rss/hard` |

## Auto-Discovery

UpWord supports RSS auto-discovery. If you use a modern RSS reader (like Feedly, Inoreader) or a browser extension, simply enter our homepage URL:

```
https://upword.day
```

Your reader will automatically detect all available feeds and allow you to choose which one to subscribe to.

## Feed Format

Our feeds allow standard RSS 2.0 format. Each item represents an article variation and includes:

- **Title**: Prefixed with difficulty level (e.g., `[Easy] Article Title`)
- **Link**: Direct link to the article reading page
- **Description**: Brief summary
- **Publication Date**: The date the article was generated
